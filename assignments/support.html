Outdoor Catering Services

We provide Outdoor catering services to organize all types of functions including Weddings, Corporate dinners, Special Birthdays and Cocktail parties. We make your event successful through our wide spread menu selection of indian cuisine by our expert master chefs. Our specialists will take care of all your requirement by catering right up to your door steps.


Pick-Up and Delivery

If you're planning an event at your house or another location--whether it's a small get-together or a large party--you can make use of our pick-up and delivery service! Your order will come conveniently packaged in our disposable to-go containers, and it will also include disposable plates, napkins, and utensils.
