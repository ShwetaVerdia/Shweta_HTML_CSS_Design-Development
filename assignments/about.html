Welcome to Rajasthan's Delight

Rajasthan's Delight is an Indian restaurant which embarks you on an exotic journey of Rajasthan, a state of India, which is famous for its rich heritage,culture and tradition. We offer diners a great tantalizing blend of traditional and contemporary flavors with mouth-watering authentic Indian food specialized in Rajasthani Platters which is rare to find in Silicon Vallley..



A Royal themed restaurant with intricate Rajasthani Mandana hand wall paintings, roof lanterns and wood ceiling replete in traditional heritage theme. The restaurant is set to give you a pleasant folksy ambience yet luxurious and fully carpeted. The ethnic Rajasthan's Delight restaurant serves Indian, Continental and Chinese cuisines. A perfect place to host a social gathering, an event or a great meet up place you can always choose Rajasthan's Delight. Come and feel the Smell,Tradition and Royality of Rajasthan.We invite you to join for an amazing dining experience.


Lunch Timings: Mon-Sun : 11 am - 3 pm
Dinner Timings: Mon-Sun : 7 pm - 11 pm
