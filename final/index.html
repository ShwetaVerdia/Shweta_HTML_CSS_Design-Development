Summer Getaways: Best Vacations

Summer vacations are always considered to be the reason of joy and happiness, as i eagerly wait for these vacations. I had my most memorable vacation. We flew from Mumbai to Bali,Indonesia. We stayed there for 3 days, and then flew to Kuala Lumpur,Malysia. And last destination was Mauritius.


Bali,Indonesia
Bali is one of the most famous destinations in the world. We explored the enchanting island province, Water Sports and a traditional Balinese massage. We did city tour of Denpasar, the capital city of the Bali province that covers Bali Museum where you can marvel at its collection of old and modern artifacts as well as the art centre Werdhi Budaya. http://www.tripadvisor.com/Attractions-g294226-Activities-Bali.html


Kuala Lumpur,Malyasia
Kuala Lumpur is a sprawling city and its residential suburbs seem to go on forever.Kuala Lumpur has grown from a small sleepy Chinese tin-mining village to a bustling metropolis. We visited the tourist attractions of the city including Malaysian handicrafts, Independent Square, Chinatown, King Palace, National Monument, Museum, Railway Station and National Mosque. http://www.kuala-lumpur.ws/attractions/10-things-to-see.htm


Mauritius
Mauritius, a sparkling crystal in the turquoise waters of the Indian Ocean, will fascinated us. The contrast of colours, cultures and tastes makes the island so charming that the scene is set for an unforgettable holiday. We visited Port Lious, North Island and South Island, Light House Of Albion, Domaine Les Pailles. http://www.mauritius-guide.net/places-to-visit/


What's New?

We left our hearts in every places we went.It was Great Experience and a very memorable trip for lifetime.
